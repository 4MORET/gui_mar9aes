# Donations & Support

Thank you for considering supporting this project. Contributions help cover hosting, domain costs, and development time. Below are suggested channels and placeholders — please replace placeholders with your actual accounts/links.

## Recommended Options

1) GitHub Sponsors (recommended)
- Setup: https://github.com/sponsors
- Placeholder badge (replace `YOUR_GITHUB`):

  [![GitHub Sponsors](https://img.shields.io/github/sponsors/YOUR_GITHUB?style=social)](https://github.com/sponsors/YOUR_GITHUB)

2) Open Collective
- Good for community-managed finances and transparency.
- Placeholder link: https://opencollective.com/YOUR_COLLECTIVE

3) Ko-fi / Buy Me a Coffee
- Simple one-time donations.
- Placeholder: https://ko-fi.com/YOUR_ACCOUNT or https://www.buymeacoffee.com/YOUR_ACCOUNT

4) PIX (Brazil)
- You can add a PIX key or instructions here for local donors.
- Example placeholder: PIX key: `your-pix-key@example.com`

## How to Add Links
- Replace the placeholder links and badges above with your actual sponsor/donation URLs.
- Add instructions for recurring vs one-time contributions and any benefits for tiers.

## Note on Safety & Transparency
- Do not store payment credentials or private keys in this repository.
- Keep donor data private and follow local regulations for receiving donations.
